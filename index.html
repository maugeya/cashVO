Rachel Cash

Voice over artist

I am a London based artist with a home studio. I'm also a member of the Voice Over Network.

My natural voice is a light-hearted clear RP with a smidge of class. Most suited for Corporate, Commerical or Narration work.

When I am not behind the mic, I am making puppets - yes puppets, for children's theatre - my animation reel will be coming soon.

About

Originally from Dorset Rachel moved up to London to study acting and through the course she found a passion for Voice-Over and also Puppetry. After graduating she worked on numerous projects for both theatre and TV including BBC’s Horrible Histories but now has moved those skills into the world of Voice-Over.


When she’s not in her booth, she can be found building puppets, being arty and making a mess in her lounge.
